By using our website or submitting your information through our intake form, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not submit your information.

01

Information We Collect

When you submit our contact or strategy call intake form, we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Full name — first and last name
  • Email address — used to respond to your inquiry and send confirmations
  • Phone number — used to follow up regarding your strategy call
  • Business name — to understand who we may be working with
  • Industry — to prepare relevant insights before your call
  • Primary advertising platform — Facebook, Instagram, or both
  • Referral source — how you found us, if provided
  • Message or challenge description — any additional context you choose to share
  • Website goals and preferences — if you are inquiring about website design services, any details you share about your desired site

We do not collect payment information, government ID, or any sensitive personal data through our website.

02

How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact form submissions — when you fill out and submit our strategy call intake form
  • Calendly scheduling — when you book a call through our embedded Calendly calendar, Calendly collects your name, email, and selected time on our behalf
  • Cookies and analytics — our website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with the site (see Section 7)
03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Responding to your inquiry and confirming your strategy call appointment
  • Preparing a personalized audit and recommendations for your call
  • Sending appointment reminders or follow-up communications related to your call
  • Improving our services based on the types of businesses and challenges we encounter

We will never use your information to send unsolicited marketing emails, sell your data, or contact you for purposes unrelated to your inquiry.

04

How We Store & Protect Your Information

Form submissions are transmitted via Formspree, a secure third-party form processing service, and delivered directly to our business email inbox at wealthforgeco@outlook.com, hosted by Microsoft Outlook. Appointment data is stored within Calendly and synced to our Outlook calendar.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to avoid sharing sensitive financial or personal data through our contact form.

We retain your submitted information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of your inquiry or as required by applicable law. If you request deletion of your data, we will make reasonable efforts to remove it from our records.

05

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

  • Formspree (formspree.io) — processes and delivers contact form submissions. View their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • Calendly (calendly.com) — handles appointment scheduling and calendar sync. View their privacy policy at calendly.com/privacy
  • Google Translate — provides optional website translation. Google may collect data per their privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Sitedrop.ai — used to design and build client websites as part of our website design service. View their privacy policy at sitedrop.ai
  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces for the site. Google may log the request per their policies

These services operate under their own privacy policies. Wealth Forge Co is not responsible for the data practices of third-party providers.

06

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

We may disclose your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our business (such as Formspree and Calendly), solely for the purpose of delivering services to you
  • If required by law, regulation, or a valid legal process such as a court order or subpoena
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Wealth Forge Co, our clients, or the public
07

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your experience. This may include:

  • Functional cookies — remembering your language preference if you use our translation feature
  • Analytics cookies — understanding how visitors navigate the site so we can improve it

Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the site. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites or serve you targeted advertising.

08

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you are located, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal information from our records
  • Opt-out — opt out of any future communications from us at any time by replying "unsubscribe" to any email

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at wealthforgeco@outlook.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

09

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business owners and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our form, please contact us immediately at wealthforgeco@outlook.com and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the Last Updated date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please reach out to us directly.
